Project information

  • Category: MOU
  • Client: Globe Biotech Limited
  • Project date: June 22, 2022
  • Project URL: www.globe-biotech.com

GenVac has been appointed as the marketing channel partner of Globe Biotech Limited for the products, services and facilitating to cunduct clinical trials on different parts of the world.

Globe Biotech Limited is engaged in the business of researching, developing and producing various medicines and vaccines, and also conducting various medical trials of such medicines and vaccines. Globe Biotech has appointed GenVac (BD) Limited as marketing channel partner for the sales and distribution of its Products and Services anywhere in the world, and for facilitating the conduct of clinical trials worldwide as and when required. Marketing Channel Partner Agreement was signed by and between Globe Biotech Limited and GenVac (BD) Limited on June 22, 2022 at Globe Biotech Limited, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Mr. Md. Harunur Rashid, Chairman, Globe Biotech Limited and Mr. Rasheq Rahman, Managing Director, GenVac (BD) Limited have signed the agreement with witness of Mr. Ahmed Hossain, Director, Globe Biotech Limited and Dr. Mamun Al Mahtab, Professor, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University in the presence of Dr. Kakon Nag, CEO and Dr. Naznin Sultana, COO, Globe Biotech Limited, and Mr. Helal Uddin, Managing Director, Clinical Research Organization Ltd.
